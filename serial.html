Serial Programming Guide for POSIX Operating Systems

The Serial Programming Guide for POSIX Operating Systems (SPG-POS) describes how to communicate with traditional RS-232 and USB TTY devices from your C or C++ program using standard POSIX APIs. The SPG-POS is available in three formats:

  • Free HTML e-Book (89k)
  • Downloadable PDF e-Book for $9.99 US through Lulu.com
  • Printed 50-page softcover book for $19.98 US + shipping and handling through Lulu.com

The SPG-POS is provided under the terms of the GNU Free Documentation License.
