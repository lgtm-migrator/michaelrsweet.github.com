"Unfinished" by Michael R Sweet

There is
        no truth,
only belief.

There is
        no judgement of others,
only self-judgement.

There is
        no unhappiness,
only happiness not chosen.

There is
        no death,
only life not lived.

-- Michael R Sweet, LTD2 January 2004
